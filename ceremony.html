The Department of Information Technology organized the inauguration of its official technical club, Eventhra, on 18th March 2025. The event aimed to kickstart a journey focused on learning, collaboration, and tech-driven innovation. The ceremony was followed by an engaging guest lecture by Mr. Maharajan Veerabahu, Co-Founder of e-con Systems & VisAI Labs, who shared his insights on Embedded Systems for Computer Vision and AI. His session gave students a better understanding of how embedded systems play a key role in AI applications and encouraged them to approach technology with fresh perspectives. With over 90 students participating, the event set a great tone for the activities and initiatives that Eventhra will lead in the future.
