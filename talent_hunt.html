Talent Hunt

Event Information

Friday, August 22, 2025

Free

Theme: Spectrum

Description

Showcase your creativity through artwork or innovative project prototypes!

Detailed Information

Eventhra Club invites all 2nd and 3rd year IT students to participate in Talent Hunt, a platform to express your creativity through art or demonstrate your problem-solving skills with innovative tech projects. A jury member from Smart India Hackathon (SIH) will personally review all submissions and deliver a guest lecture on creativity and innovation. Participants are required to submit a photo of their artwork or a short video/image of their project prototype in advance for review.

Event Rules

  • Individual participation only
  • Artwork must be original
  • Project prototypes must be student-built
  • Submissions must be sent on or before 10 August 2025
